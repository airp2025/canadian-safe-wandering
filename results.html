You are not alone. Many people living with dementia are at a medium risk. Below are some strategies you can try to reduce your risk of getting lost. It is better to have at least two strategies in place in case one fails.

Even though you are at this risk level right now, you can be at a greater or lesser level of risk at any moment. For example, one moment you know exactly where you are, and a few minutes later you suddenly are lost and disoriented. Because of this, look at all three risk levels so that you can act quickly when your risk level does change.

Remember that your risk might change over time, so please consider revisiting this website in the future.
